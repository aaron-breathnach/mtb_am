extract protocol	AM were infected with live virulent Mtb H37Rv for 24 hours (hr) using a multiplicity of infection of 1-10 bacilli per cell, as previously described. AM were then lysed, and total RNA was collected and purified using silica-membrane column centrifugation.
label protocol	NanoString
hyb protocol	NanoString nCounter Metabolic Pathways Panel 
scan protocol	NanoString nCounter Metabolic Pathways Panel 
data processing	Data were analysed using the NanoString nSolver Data Analysis Software
value definition	Counts per gene
